# Thermal-Structural Analysis of PLA/CNT/hBN Hybrid Nanocomposites

## Overview

This project investigates the coupled thermal and structural behavior of PLA/CNT/hBN hybrid nanocomposites using finite element analysis. The objective is to evaluate temperature distribution, thermal stresses, and structural performance under different loading conditions.

---

## Objective

To analyze the thermo-mechanical performance of hybrid polymer nanocomposites and identify the optimum material composition for engineering applications.

---

## Software Used

- ANSYS Workbench
- ANSYS Mechanical

---

## Methodology

- Developed the finite element model.
- Assigned material properties for hybrid composites.
- Applied thermal and structural boundary conditions.
- Performed coupled thermal-structural analysis.
- Evaluated stress, deformation, and temperature distribution.

---

## Results

- Predicted thermal distribution.
- Evaluated structural deformation.
- Compared different composite compositions.
- Identified improved thermo-mechanical performance.
